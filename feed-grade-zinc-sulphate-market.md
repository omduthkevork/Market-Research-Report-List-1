<p><ul><li>Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate</li><li>Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The feed grade zinc sulfate market primarily consists of two types: Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate and Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate. Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate is a crystalline compound containing seven water molecules, making it highly soluble and commonly used in animal feed to enhance zinc levels. Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate, on the other hand, contains one water molecule and is often favored for its higher zinc concentration per unit weight. Both forms serve crucial roles in livestock nutrition and agricultural applications, ensuring efficient mineral supplementation.</p></p>
