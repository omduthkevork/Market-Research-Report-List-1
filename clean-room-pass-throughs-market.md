<p><p>Clean room pass throughs are essential for maintaining sterile environments in industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology. The market is divided into two primary styles: wall-mounted and floor-mounted. Wall-mounted pass throughs are installed on existing walls, optimizing space in smaller clean rooms, while floor-mounted models provide larger, more accessible transfer areas and are suitable for high-volume material handling. Both styles are designed to minimize contamination, ensuring a controlled transfer of materials between clean spaces and adjacent non-clean areas.</p></p>
