<p><p>Servo-drives and servo-amplifiers are essential components in automation systems, providing precise control of motor functions. Servo-drives are responsible for converting control signals into the appropriate power levels for the motor, ensuring accurate movement and positioning. Servo-amplifiers enhance this process by boosting the power transmitted to the motors, which is critical for high-performance applications such as robotics, CNC machines, and conveyor systems.</p><p>The market for servo-drives and servo-amplifiers is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for automation in industries like manufacturing, automotive, and electronics. Advances in technology, such as the integration of IoT capabilities, are enhancing system efficiency and enabling predictive maintenance.
