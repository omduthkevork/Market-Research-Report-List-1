<p><p>Silicon impression materials are essential in dentistry for capturing precise dental impressions. The market is divided into two main types: light body and medium to heavy body materials. Light body materials are used for detailed areas, as they flow easily around anatomical structures and capture fine details, making them ideal for complete and partial impressions. In contrast, medium to heavy body materials provide additional viscosity and firmness, making them suitable for overall impressions where stability and support are needed.</p></p>
