<p><ul><li>Aluminum Radiator</li><li>Copper Radiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) radiator market encompasses two primary types: aluminum and copper radiators. Aluminum radiators are lightweight, cost-effective, and resistant to corrosion, making them popular for electric and hybrid vehicles. They offer efficient thermal management and contribute to overall vehicle performance. Conversely, copper radiators, known for superior heat conductivity, ensure effective cooling even in high-performance applications. They tend to be heavier and more expensive, appealing to niche markets focusing on performance and reliability. Both types play a crucial role in NEV efficiency.</p></p>
