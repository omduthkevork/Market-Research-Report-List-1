<p><ul><li>PIR (Passive Infrared) Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Sensors</li><li>Dual-technology Occupancy Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting systems with occupancy sensors utilize various technologies to enhance energy efficiency and convenience. PIR (Passive Infrared) sensors detect motion based on body heat, making them effective for spaces with clear line of sight. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to sense motion, enabling detection through obstacles. Dual-technology sensors combine PIR and ultrasonic methods for increased accuracy and reduced false triggers. Other sensors may include microwave or photoelectric types, catering to diverse environments and specific user needs, enhancing overall energy management.</p></p>
