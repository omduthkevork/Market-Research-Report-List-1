<p><p>The venous catheter market is categorized into several types based on the number of lumens. Single-lumen catheters are designed for simple infusions or blood draws. Double-lumen catheters allow simultaneous medication delivery and blood withdrawal, enhancing efficiency. Triple-lumen catheters provide three access points for multiple therapies, beneficial in complex clinical situations. Other market options may include specialized catheters designed for specific purposes, such as dialysis or long-term access, catering to diverse medical needs and improving patient care.</p></p>
