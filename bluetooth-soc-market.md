<p><p>The Bluetooth SoC market comprises three primary types: Single Mode, Dual-Mode, and Other. Single Mode SoCs support only Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), ideal for power-sensitive applications like wearables. Dual-Mode SoCs handle both classic Bluetooth and BLE, making them versatile for devices requiring robust data transfer and low energy consumption. The "Other" category includes specialized solutions, such as those for audio streaming or integrated with Wi-Fi, catering to niche markets and advanced functionalities, enhancing overall connectivity options.</p></p>
