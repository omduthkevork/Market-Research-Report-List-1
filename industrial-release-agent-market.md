<p><strong>The Industrial Release Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Release Agent</li><li>External Release Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial release agent market is segmented into internal and external release agents. Internal release agents are incorporated into the materials during production, enhancing separation from molds during processing, and improving product quality. In contrast, external release agents are applied on the surface of molds or products before processing, creating a barrier that facilitates easy demolding. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, such as plastics, rubber, and concrete, ensuring efficient manufacturing and reducing operational costs.</p></p>
