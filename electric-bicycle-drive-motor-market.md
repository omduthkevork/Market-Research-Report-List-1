<p><ul><li>Hub Motor</li><li>Mid Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bicycle drive motor market primarily features two types: hub motors and mid motors. Hub motors are integrated into the wheel hub, providing direct power and simpler installation, making them popular for casual riders. In contrast, mid motors are positioned near the bike's crank, allowing for better weight distribution and improved handling, often favored by performance-oriented cyclists. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to diverse rider preferences and requirements, contributing to the growing appeal of electric bicycles.</p></p>
