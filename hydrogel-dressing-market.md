<p><p>Hydrogel dressing is a type of wound care product made from a water-based polymer that provides a moist healing environment, promoting faster healing and reducing pain at the wound site. As healthcare providers increasingly recognize the advantages of maintaining a moist wound healing environment, demand for hydrogel dressings continues to rise. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period. </p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift toward advanced hydrogel formulations, incorporating antimicrobial properties and enhanced adhesive technologies, improving the dressing's performance. Furthermore, the rise of outpatient care and home healthcare settings is expanding the usage of hydrogel dressings, catering to patients' needs in non-hospital environments. These factors are contributing to the overall expansion and innovation in the hydrogel dressing market.</p></p>
