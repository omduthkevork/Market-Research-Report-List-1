<p><p>The image fire detector market consists of various types, primarily including ionization detector types and photoelectric detector types. Ionization detectors utilize radioactive material to sense smoke particles, making them highly effective for fast-flaming fires. Photoelectric detectors, on the other hand, use a light source and a sensor to detect the presence of smoke, making them better for smoldering fires. Additionally, other types may include specialized systems or hybrid detectors that combine both technologies for enhanced fire detection capabilities.</p></p>
