<p><ul><li>Photochemical</li><li>Catalytic Wet</li><li>Sonochemical</li><li>Ozone</li><li>Electrochemical</li><li>Fenton</li><li>Complex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AOP (Advanced Oxidation Processes) systems are categorized into several market types based on the technology used for wastewater treatment. Photochemical AOP utilizes UV light to generate hydroxyl radicals, while Catalytic Wet AOP involves catalysts to enhance oxidation. Sonochemical AOP leverages ultrasound for chemical reactions, and Ozone AOP uses ozone gas for oxidation. Electrochemical AOP employs electrical currents, whereas Fenton AOP involves iron catalysts and hydrogen peroxide. The Complex market combines various AOP technologies for enhanced efficiency.</p></p>
