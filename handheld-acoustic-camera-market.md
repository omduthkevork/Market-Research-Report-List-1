<p><p>The handheld acoustic camera market includes two primary types: 2D microphone arrays and 3D microphone arrays. 2D microphone arrays capture sound information in a planar format, useful for identifying sound sources and mapping noise levels in a specific area. In contrast, 3D microphone arrays provide spatial sound data, enabling more precise localization of sound sources in three dimensions. This added depth enhances applications in various fields such as industrial diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and research, facilitating detailed sound analysis and visualization.</p></p>
