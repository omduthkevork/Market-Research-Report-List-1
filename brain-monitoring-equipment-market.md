<p><p>The brain monitoring equipment market encompasses various devices used to assess and monitor brain activity and health. Electroencephalography (EEG) devices measure electrical activity in the brain, while Magnetoencephalography (MEG) detects magnetic fields produced by neural activity. Transcranial Doppler (TCD) devices assess blood flow in the brain. Intracranial Pressure (ICP) monitors track pressure inside the skull. Cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels, while MRI and CT devices provide detailed images of brain structures. Sleep monitoring devices assess sleep patterns and disturbances.</p></p>
