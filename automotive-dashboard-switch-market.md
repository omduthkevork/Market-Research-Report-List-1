<p><p>The automotive dashboard switch market encompasses various switch types, notably button-type and induction-type switches. Button-type switches are mechanical switches activated by pressing, providing tactile feedback and reliable operation for functions like lights and wipers. In contrast, induction-type switches utilize capacitive sensing to detect user gestures without direct contact, enhancing aesthetics and safety. Both types serve essential roles in vehicle controls, with button-type switches favored for their simplicity and durability, while induction-type switches offer modern, sleek designs and advanced functionality.</p></p>
