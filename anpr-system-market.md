Mobile ANPR systems are mounted on vehicles, allowing for dynamic monitoring of traffic and enforcement. Portable ANPR systems are handheld devices used by law enforcement for on-the-spot checks. Each type caters to specific operational needs, enhancing vehicle tracking and security across various environments.</p></p>
