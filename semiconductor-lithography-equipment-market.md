<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Lithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>KrF</li><li>I-Line</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>EUV</li><li>ArF Immersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor lithography equipment market is classified into several types based on the light source used for patterning. KrF (Krypton Fluoride) is suitable for producing 180nm and larger nodes. I-Line operates at 365nm, primarily for older technologies. ArF (Argon Fluoride) Dry is used for finer resolutions, typically 130nm to 45nm nodes, while EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) offers even smaller patterning down to 7nm and below. ArF Immersion uses a liquid medium to enhance resolution, improving performance for advanced manufacturing.</p></p>
