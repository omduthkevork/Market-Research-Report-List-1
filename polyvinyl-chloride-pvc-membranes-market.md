<p><p>The PVC membranes market is primarily divided into plasticized and unplasticized PVC membranes. Plasticized PVC membranes are flexible and widely used in applications like roofing and waterproofing due to their enhanced elasticity. They contain added plasticizers that improve workability and adaptability. In contrast, unplasticized PVC membranes are rigid and offer superior tensile strength and durability, making them ideal for applications requiring stability, such as window frames and pipes. Both types are essential for various construction and industrial applications, each serving distinct needs.</p></p>
