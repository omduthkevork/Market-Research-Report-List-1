<p><strong>The Aircraft Magnetic Compass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Compass</li><li>Solid State Compass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Magnetic Compass market is primarily divided into two types: liquid compasses and solid-state compasses. Liquid compasses utilize a fluid-filled design that stabilizes the magnetic needle, providing reliable heading information even in turbulent conditions. Solid-state compasses, on the other hand, employ advanced electronic sensors and microprocessors to deliver precise, real-time navigation data without the moving parts found in liquid compasses. Both types serve critical roles in aviation navigation, offering varying levels of accuracy and reliability depending on operational needs.</p></p>
