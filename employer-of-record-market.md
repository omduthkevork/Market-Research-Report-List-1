<p><p>The Employer of Record (EOR) market consists of two primary models: the Aggregator Model and the Wholly Owned Infrastructure Model. </p><p>In the Aggregator Model, multiple EORs partner to leverage shared resources and provide comprehensive services, enhancing scalability and market reach without heavy infrastructure investment. Conversely, the Wholly Owned Infrastructure Model involves a single EOR managing its own staff, payroll, compliance, and benefits, allowing for greater control and customization but requiring significant investment in infrastructure and operational capabilities.</p></p>
