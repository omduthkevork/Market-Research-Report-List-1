<p><strong>The Iron and Steel Slag Processing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Splash Method</li><li>Hot Stuffy Method</li><li>Roller Method</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron and steel slag processing involves various methods to recycle and utilize slag by-products. The Hot Splash Method involves rapidly cooling molten slag to create coarse aggregates. The Hot Stuffy Method allows for controlled cooling and stabilization of slag through heating. The Roller Method processes cooled slag using rollers, transforming it into fine aggregates for construction. Other methods may include granulation or air-cooling techniques, tailored to specific applications in construction, cement production, and road development, maximizing resource efficiency.</p></p>
