<p><strong>The Construction Motor Grader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Frame Motor Grader</li><li>Articulated Frame Motor Grader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Construction Motor Grader market comprises two main types: Rigid Frame Motor Graders and Articulated Frame Motor Graders. Rigid Frame Motor Graders feature a fixed chassis, offering enhanced stability and strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications and straightforward grading tasks. In contrast, Articulated Frame Motor Graders possess a pivot point between the front and rear sections, allowing for improved maneuverability and flexibility in tight spaces, making them ideal for diverse terrains and complex grading operations.</p></p>
