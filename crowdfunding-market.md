<p><p>Crowdfunding is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Reward-based Crowdfunding** offers backers tangible rewards, such as products or services, in exchange for their financial support. </p><p>2. **Equity Crowdfunding** allows investors to acquire shares in the business, sharing in its potential profits and growth. </p><p>3. **Donation-based Crowdfunding** is purely philanthropic, where supporters contribute without expecting returns. </p><p>4. **Other crowdfunding models** can include revenue-based financing or debt crowdfunding, where backers receive a share of future revenues or interest on loans. Each type serves distinct funding needs.</p></p>
