<p><p>Fire protection sprinkler systems are categorized into active and passive types. Active systems involve components that respond directly to fire conditions, such as water-based sprinklers that automatically activate to suppress flames. In contrast, passive systems are designed to contain or slow the spread of fire without intervention, using fire-resistant materials and barriers. Both types are essential for comprehensive fire safety, addressing different aspects of fire protection through proactive suppression and structural integrity. Their integration enhances overall safety in buildings.</p></p>
