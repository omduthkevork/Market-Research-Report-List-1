<p><p>Polyisobutylene (PIB) is categorized into three market types based on molecular weight: low, medium, and high. Low Molecular Weight Polyisobutylene is primarily used as an adhesive and sealant due to its tackiness and flexibility. Medium Molecular Weight PIB is utilized in lubricants and coatings for its enhanced viscosity. High Molecular Weight Polyisobutylene offers excellent gas barrier properties, making it ideal for applications in the automotive and packaging industries. Each variant serves distinct purposes, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
