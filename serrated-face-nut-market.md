<p><p>The serrated face nut market is categorized into two main types: single-side serrated nuts and double-sided serrated nuts. Single-side serrated nuts feature serrations on one face, providing a firm grip during fastening, commonly used in applications with limited space. In contrast, double-sided serrated nuts have serrations on both faces, offering enhanced locking capabilities and stability, ideal for high-vibration environments. Both types are essential in industries requiring secure assemblies, such as automotive, machinery, and construction, ensuring durability and performance.</p></p>
